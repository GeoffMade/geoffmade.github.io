About Geoff

With a rich experience spanning just under a decade, Geoff is a name synonymous with precision, creativity, and mastery in the realm of glass artistry. Hailing from Austin, Texas, his journey commenced at Flameworks studio, under the mentorship of seasoned artisans Jmass and Kevin Ivey.

Every creation of 'Geoff Made Glass' tells a tale of dedication and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Whether it’s his luxurious yet familiarly shaped cups, meticulously crafted functional pieces for cannabis enthusiasts, or bespoke glass attachments for electronic rigs, Geoff’s work is a blend of technical finesse and heartfelt passion. His preference for working on the lathe, as opposed to the bench, unveils a unique dance of creation, bringing forth pieces that are both aesthetically pleasing and functionally superior.
