About Geoff

Since embarking on his artistic journey in 2010, Geoff Madely has meticulously honed his skills in borosilicate glass artistry, with the foundations laid at Flameworks studio in Austin, Texas under the mentorship of Jmass and Kevin Ivery, he delved into mastering the precise and enthralling craft that now defines his repertoire.

Geoff’s work seamlessly blends elegance with functionality, whether it’s through his tasteful cups, crafted functional pieces for cannabis aficionados, or bespoke glass attachments for electronic dab rigs. His preferred realm of creation is at the Vertigo lathe, where each spin transforms glass into functional art, embodying a blend of aesthetic allure and practical utility, hallmarking the essence of Geoff Made’s craftsmanship.
